Mercury

Facts:

From the surface of Mercury, the Sun would appear more than three times as large as it does when viewed from Earth, and the sunlight would be as much as 11 times brighter.

Fun Facts:

  • Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and is the closest planet to our sun.
  • Mercury is the fastest planet in our solar system and is named after the fastest of the Roman Gods.
