Venus

Facts:

Venus is the hottest planet in our solar system due to its thick atmosphere filled with carbon dioxide, and vicinity to the sun. It’s even hotter than the closest planet to the sun, Mercury. Venus remains about 38 million miles from Earth on average, though most of the time Earth and Venus are farther apart; in comparison, Mercury actually spends more time in Earth’s proximity than Venus.

Fun Facts:

  • Venus has no moons, and no rings.
